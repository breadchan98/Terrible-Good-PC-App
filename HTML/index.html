Crap PC Build Lists checklist

Things to avoid

  • Cluttered cable/lack of cable management
  • Unnecessary RGB Lighting
  • Incorrect RAM Placement
  • Expensive cooling with a cheap GPU/CPU
  • Giant PC Case with small components

Top 3 things to have good PC Build

  1. Focus on cable management, make it presentable
  2. Dont block any fans with cluttered cable, at least
  3. Manage the RGB and components correctly
